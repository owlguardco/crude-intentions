## Notes

- **Existing `/api/fvg-scan`** is the manual JSON-driven path used by `FVGScanWidget`. **`/api/fvg-scan-auto`** is the new server-fetched scorer with its own KV key (`market:fvg_scan`). The two are intentionally additive — do not merge without a migration plan for `MarketContext.active_fvgs`.
- **Two MTF systems coexist** by design: `mtf_consensus` (macro, Daily/4H/1H weighted, persisted in journal entries) and `entry_alignment` (tactical, HTF/setup EMA stacks vs. trigger, response-only). Downstream consumers must keep them distinct.
- **`historical: true`** on a journal entry excludes it from cohort calculations (`by_grade`, `by_session`, `by_confidence`, `by_factor`, `by_supply_bias`) but **counts** in `totals.trades_closed`, `overall.win_rate`, and `rolling_30`. Guided imports stamp this automatically; raw-JSON imports must opt in.
- **CL session-open** anchors to the CME 6 PM ET roll, not wall-clock midnight (commit `51766a0`). If Yahoo's intraday data is wall-clock-dated rather than trading-day-dated, the Sunday-evening case may still return null until midnight ET — flip the `+24h` to `-24h` in `sessionDateInNY` if so.
- **Post-mortem auto-fire** runs on every close from `closeTrade()`. Bulk import passes `skipPostmortem: true` to avoid 50-trade Anthropic blasts. Webhook-close and backtest paths still auto-fire.
